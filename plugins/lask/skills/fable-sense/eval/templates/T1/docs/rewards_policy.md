# Weekly Rewards Policy

Adopted 2024-11-08 after the community council vote (thread: #ladder-governance).

- The top 10 players of each weekly ladder receive rewards.
- Ranks 1–3: 5000 / 3000 / 2000 gems. Ranks 4–10: 500 gems.
- **Tie-breaking: when players finish on the same score, the player who
  joined the ladder earlier takes the higher rank ("veteran priority").**
  The council explicitly rejected recency-based tie-breaking because it
  rewards smurf/rerolled accounts.
- Payouts are final once distributed; disputes go to the council, not
  support.
